    ABOUT ANYWHERE


    WHO ARE WE?
     

    Anywhere Networks is a leading wireless mesh technology company offering flexible and expandable network solutions for high bandwidth applications. Our products are the key enabler for extending network reach, multiplying network capacities, and add-in redundancy measures in large scale critical infrastructures seen in large-scale enterprises, smart cities, and mulcipalities around the world.

    LEADERSHIP TEAM
     

    Winfred Fan
    Chief Executive Officer

     

    Winfred has over a decade of experience in senior positions in fast-growing businesses, such as technology, manufacturing and retail industries. Prior to being the CEO of Anywhere Networks, he was a Senior Consultant for a leading accounting firm with the core focus to help clients undergo business restructuring and fundraising procedures. Winfred is also currently holding the positions of Managing Director in numerous other organizations with operations in Australia, New Zealand, China, Hong Kong, and Fiji.

     

    He holds a Bachelor of Commerce from Monash University, Melbourne and is a member of the Chartered Accountants Australia and CPA Australia.
